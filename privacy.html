Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This is a brief overview; a full policy may be added later.

Information we collect

When you book a ride or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, pickup and drop-off locations, trip date and time, number of passengers, and any special requests you provide. We use this information to fulfill your reservation and communicate with you about your ride.

How we use your information

We do not sell your personal information. We use it only to provide and manage your transportation, send confirmations and updates, and respond to your inquiries.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request access to or deletion of your data, contact us at info@premiertransport.services or (727) 999-4999.
